Packing Focus

  • One checked bag under 50 pounds plus one carry-on/daypack.
  • Closed-toe shoes outside accommodations; sturdy shoes or boots for Las Tablas mangrove/reforestation work.
  • Raincoat or umbrella, SPF 50+ sunscreen, and daily mosquito repellent.
  • $100-$200 in small US bills, plus card or Apple/Google Pay for Newark.

Rules to Know

  • No tobacco, alcohol, illegal drugs, sexual behavior/coupling, new tattoos, or piercings.
  • No Wi-Fi-capable electronics in-country; leaders store phones.
  • Quiet time 9:00 PM, in rooms 10:30 PM, lights out 11:00 PM.
  • Open-water swimming is waist-deep only without a life vest, never alone.
